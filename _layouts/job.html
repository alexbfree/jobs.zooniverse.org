About the Zooniverse

The Zooniverse is home to the Internet's largest, most popular and most successful citizen science projects, allowing anyone to become part of real research online. Our back-end systems are built in Ruby on Rails and deployed to Amazon Web Services; our front-end stack includes React, Spine, Angular and Stylus.

{% if page.location == 'Oxford' %}

We're a team of around 12 based at in the centre of Oxford, at the University of Oxford's Department of Physics. The wider Zooniverse team is spread across Oxford, the Adler Planetarium in Chicago, and the University of Minnesota.
